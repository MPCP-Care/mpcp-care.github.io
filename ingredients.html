AMLA BENEFITS

Healthy benefits :-

  • Reduce dry skin
  • Improve brain health and function
  • reduce premature pigment loss from hair, or greying
  • Reduce the appearance of scars, blemishes, age spots, and wrinkles

    • Tips-

    Silk advance Amla oil is often used in place of a conditioner after shampooing. It is gently massaged into the scalp so that it lightly coats the hair. People with an oily scalp should apply it to the hair shafts only. After 15 minutes or so, thoroughly rinse the hair with warm water. If amla oil gets into the eyes, rinse thoroughly with cool water.

    Silk advance Amla oilcan also be left on the scalp overnight for deep conditioning. If used in this way, wear a shower cap and cover your pillows with towels to prevent staining. Amla oil can have a strong, musky odor, so you may want to use it sparingly if applied overnight.

    COCONUT BENEFITS

    Healthy benefits :-

  • Boost hair and skin health
  • May boost heart health
  • May encourage fat burning
  • May raise HDL (good) cholesterol

    • Tips-

    Mix one cup of hot water and two tablespoons of Silk coconut oil (use more if your hair is longer). Once the Silk coconut oil settles, massage it onto your scalp and hair, comb it through with a wide-toothed comb, wrap it into a bun, and put a shower cap over it. Let it sit for an hour, then rinse thoroughly with warm water for shiny, beautiful hair!

    GINGER BENEFITS

    Healthy benefits :-

  • Fade Scars And Marks
  • Anti Ageing Mask
  • Hair Growth

    • Tips-

    Ginger is affective in promoting hair growth. The combination of vitamins, minerals and fatty acids present in ginger strengthen hair follicles and combats hair loss. Similarly, it also improves blood circulation through your scalp that stimulates hair growth. Also, it has antiseptic properties that eliminate and cure dandruff. Mix ginger juice with 3 tablespoons of Silk coconut. Massage it and leave it for half an hour before washing your hair. Repeat this every time before a hair wash for better results. It also treats split ends along with dry and brittle hair.

    HONEY BENEFITS

    Healthy benefits :-

  • Hair Conditioner
  • Face Cleanser
  • Getting Rid of Acne and Blemishes
  • Treating Sunburn

    • Tips-

    After you shampoo your hair, apply honey from the root to the tip and let it sit for three to four minutes, if not more, before rinsing with lukewarm water. You will immediately see the result. You can also mix it with a little Silk coconut oil and apply on your hair before shampooing to tame frizzy hair.
